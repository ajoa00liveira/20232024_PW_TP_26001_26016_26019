Our Story

The Origin Museum was founded in 2018 with a vision to showcase the rich history and cultural heritage of civilizations across the globe. Since its inception, the museum has undergone several expansions and renovations to accommodate new themes and exhibits.

Art Gallery

Built in 2018, the Art Gallery features a stunning collection of paintings, sculptures, and artifacts from various periods of art history.

Egyptian Exhibit

The Egyptian Exhibit, established in 2020, transports visitors back in time to ancient Egypt, showcasing mummies, hieroglyphics, and treasures from the pharaohs.

Fossil & Pre-Historic Collection

In 2022, the Fossil Collection was unveiled, presenting a journey through the prehistoric world with dinosaur skeletons, fossils, and interactive exhibits.
