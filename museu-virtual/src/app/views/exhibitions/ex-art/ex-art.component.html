Exhibitions of Art

See the most famous attractions in each exhibition in the Art division

Classical and Ancient Art

This exhibition transports visitors back to the ancient times of Greece and Rome, featuring works of art that reflect the beliefs, values ​​and mythologies of these ancient civilizations. From Greek vases adorned with mythological scenes to enigmatic sculptures, the artifacts on display offer a fascinating window into the distant past and the richness of artistic expression of these ancient cultures.

Greek Vase: A Greek ceramic vase from the Classical period, decorated with mythological scenes and intricate patterns, representing the art and culture of Ancient Greece.

Roman Mosaic: A fragment of a Roman mosaic that depicts scenes from everyday Roman life, such as banquets or hunting scenes, demonstrating the skill and technical sophistication of Roman artists.

"The School of Athens" - Raphael: One of the most famous works of the Renaissance, it portrays Greek philosophers such as Plato and Aristotle in a grand architectural environment, symbolizing the intellectual flowering of Antiquity.
