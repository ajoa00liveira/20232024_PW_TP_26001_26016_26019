Stay up to date with all our news and updates!

New fossil found will be displayed at the Origin Museum

A new fossil of a dinosaur that was extinct more than 252 million years ago was found in Mongolia. Researchers determined it could be a new type of predator.

1st year anniversary of the Ancient Egypt Division scheduled for July 10th!

To celebrate the 1st year anniversary of the opening of the Ancient Egypt Division, an event will be held where everyone is invited! We will have several activities and food.

Painting was restored and returned to the Origin museum!

The painting "Harvest Delight" will once again be presented at the Origin museum. The painting had left the presentation due to restoration and after a few months it will finally return on June 23rd!

Origin Museum adopts virtual reality for more immersive experiences

Museum visitors can now see representations of: "Jurassic World", "Ancient Egypt" and "Art History" through virtual reality! Available June 1, 2024. Visit Origin Museum to try it out!

Museum Launches Summer Educational Program for Children

The Origin Museum has launched a new summer educational program aimed at children ages 8 to 12. The program includes art workshops, fossil exploration sessions and activities about Egyptian culture.

Museum Organizes Themed Cinema Night

The Origin Museum is hosting a Themed Cinema Night, showing a film about Ancient Egypt. The event will be held in the museum garden, on May 22nd. There will also be the presence of experts at the end.
